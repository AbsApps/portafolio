During my time as an English student at Miami Dade College, I noticed that many of my classmates and I faced difficulties with English pronunciation. Motivated by this experience, after completing the iOS App Development course, I developed an app focused on improving English learning, especially in the area of pronunciation.

In January 2025, I presented this application at the Apple Swift Student Showcase, organized by Miami Dade College, where it was selected as the best of the event. As recognition, I was invited to present it at Apple's offices.
